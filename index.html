Shay the Full Stack Developer

Hi there! I’m Shay, a 22-year-old aspiring full-stack developer from Israel who transitioned straight from military service into the world of code. Since my discharge six months ago, I’ve been diving into HTML, CSS, JavaScript (and frameworks like React), as well as Node.js and databases on the back end. By day I balance my full-stack studies with a part-time job, and by evening you’ll find me leveling up my skills—whether it’s building small web apps, hitting the gym for a workout, or recharging by the sea. As a beginner full-stack programmer, I’m passionate about crafting clean, responsive interfaces and learning how to architect solid server-side logic. I love taking on new challenges, collaborating with others, and turning ideas into real, user-friendly projects.
